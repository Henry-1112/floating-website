Our Story

A Unique Dining Experience

La Vida Floating is a luxury riverside restaurant in Phnom Penh, Cambodia, offering a unique floating dining experience. We serve Khmer food, fresh seafood, Asian cuisine, Western dishes, and international fusion meals. Perfect for dine-in, private events, sunset river cruises, boat tours, and live music nights — all with the best sunset view in Phnom Penh and a romantic, relaxing ambiance. Visit us for the ultimate riverside experience!

Whether you're celebrating a special occasion or simply enjoying a night out, our floating venue provides an unforgettable atmosphere that enhances every dining experience.
